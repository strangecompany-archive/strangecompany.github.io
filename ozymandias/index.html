The Poem

Ozymandias is a short filmic adaption of Percy Bysshe Shelley's poem of the same name, created using the new filmic medium "Machinima", in the Lithtech Film Producer package.

It was scripted and directed by Hugh Hancock, and filmed by Strange Company, both as a film in its own right and as a demonstration of Lithtech Film Producer's potential.
