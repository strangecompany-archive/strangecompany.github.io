"Matrix 4x1" is a series of four short films originally commissioned from Strange Company by Cybernetic Productions, set in the world of Warner Bros' "The Matrix".

Showing four short scenes from the never-ending action of the Matrix universe, these sequences were made using Machinima techniques within the Half-Life 3D engine.

We're very interested in hearing comments, questions or requests about the "Matrix 4x1" project- please do email us!
